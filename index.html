Waves 2019

Everyone wants a chance. Everyone needs a chance. A chance to get a platform where their voice will rise above the screams of the crowd. Where their actions will set the tune for the crowd. Where rage will matter. But very few get to embrace that chance with open arms and then go on to be the “shining stars” of their world. Searock brings to you the opportunity to relish that open-armed embrace. With eliminations in multiple cities around India, and now eliminations in Kathmandu, Nepal. Searock gives you the chance of bringing out your thoughts, your rage, your music and placing it before the world to listen. If your music is true, then cash prizes worth 1 Lakh INR, scholarships at the best music academies of the nation, music goodies, and a feature in the leading media publications are waiting for you.

If you believe your music is true and have what it takes to go all the way, don’t let go of this chance. Register now and come closer to your dream of making the first leap into the ocean of music, of taking that first step towards success. Let us find you so that the world can get the chance to listen to you. Step out of the crowd. Be a rebel. Be a rocker!

Cheers,
The Searock Team
